# GLD UT Competition Lab

Test six UT-style ATR variants under equal Strategy Tester conditions:

1. UT Original
2. UT + EMA200
3. UT + Volume
4. UT + Session
5. UT + EMA200 + Volume
6. UT + Higher Timeframe

Use the same symbol, timeframe, date range, capital, commission, slippage, position size, stop, target, and holding period. Change only the Strategy Variant input.

Record:
- Net profit %
- Max drawdown %
- Percent profitable
- Profit factor
- Closed trades

No webhook execution and no broker credentials.
