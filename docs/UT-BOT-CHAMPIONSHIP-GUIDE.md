# UT Bot Championship

Competitors are grouped into Trend, Momentum, Volume, and Risk divisions.

Run `python research_wizard.py` to register a result and refresh the leaderboard and command-center reports.

Run `python ut_championship_manager.py` to generate `reports/ut_championship_summary.json`.

Keep symbol, timeframe, date range, capital, commission, slippage, sizing, UT sensitivity, and ATR period identical. Change only the competitor.

Research only. No broker connection, market request, order submission, or automatic promotion.
